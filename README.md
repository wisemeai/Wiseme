# WiseMe

**WiseMe** is a local-first, on-device voice-to-text input app designed for fast, private speech typing across apps.

## What is WiseMe?

WiseMe lets you turn speech into text instantly, without sending your voice to the cloud.

All speech recognition runs **locally on your device**, providing:

- Low latency
- Strong privacy protection
- Reliable offline performance

WiseMe works across apps, allowing you to use voice typing wherever you normally type.

No audio data is uploaded to the cloud during voice recognition.

Your data stays with you.
